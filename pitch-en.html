The opportunity

Chinese brands are taking Europe — and the timing is now.

  • ~6% of Europe today → ~16% by 2030 — a buyer base forming right now (AlixPartners).
  • No buyer-guidance layer exists — discovery is fragmented classifieds like Mobile.de, market by market.
  • China already won Africa, the Middle East and Asia. Europe is next — demand must be built, not just listed.

JATO H1-2025: Chinese brands hit a record 5.1% of Europe (+91% YoY); BYD +311% to 70,500 units. Battery-electric is 17.4% of EU new cars (ACEA, 2025).
